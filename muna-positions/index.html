Does the band MUNA always stand in the same order?

The band does not always stand in the same order, according to a data-driven investigation from Columbia University’s Lede Program – but they do do it a lot.

By Jade-Ruyu Yan

Earlier this month, a Twitter user noted that members of the band MUNA always appear to stand in the same order.

The order, according to X user andromeda in a Tweet on June 23, has singer Katie Gavin at the centre, with multi-instrumentalist Naomi McPherson on the left and lead guitarist Josette Maskin on the right, from the perspective of the viewer.

It’s “in literally everything, in pictures, and even on stage,” wrote andromeda. Another X user speculated that they thought it “on purpose.”

A data-driven investigation by Columbia University examined every picture the band has ever posted on Instagram, and showed that the band does not actually always stand in the same order.
